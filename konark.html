History & Significance

The Konark Sun Temple, built in the 13th century by King Narasimhadeva I, is a masterpiece of Odia architecture. Designed in the form of a colossal chariot for the Sun God Surya, it features twelve exquisitely carved stone wheels and is drawn by seven horses. The entire structure is a testimony to the brilliance of Indian artisans and engineers.
